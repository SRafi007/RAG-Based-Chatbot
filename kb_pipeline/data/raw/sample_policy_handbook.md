# Softvence Agency – Company Policy Handbook
*(Prepared for knowledge-base use in Hybrid RAG systems)*

---

## 1. Company Overview & Core Values

Softvence Agency is a digital solutions company specializing in **web design**, **AI integration**, and **software development**.  
Our goal is to empower businesses through innovative technology while maintaining a workplace that promotes **creativity**, **transparency**, and **respect**.

### Core Values

1. **Integrity** — We act honestly and ethically.  
2. **Innovation** — We encourage new ideas and continuous learning.  
3. **Respect** — We value diversity, inclusion, and mutual respect.  
4. **Collaboration** — We believe great results come from teamwork.  
5. **Accountability** — We take ownership of our work and commitments.

---

## 2. Employment & Onboarding

- **Eligibility:** Applicants must meet local employment laws and Softvence’s hiring criteria.  
- **Probationary Period:** 3 months, during which performance is reviewed regularly.  
- **Confirmation:** Successful completion results in full employment confirmation.  
- **Orientation:** New hires attend a 2-day onboarding program covering company systems, HR tools, and data security.

### Equal Employment Opportunity

Softvence prohibits discrimination based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or disability.  
All employment decisions are based on merit and company needs.

---

## 3. Working Hours, Attendance & Remote Work

- **Standard Hours:** 9 AM – 6 PM, Monday – Friday.  
- **Hybrid Work:** Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval.  
- **Flexible Schedule:** Certain roles may adopt flexible timing if agreed upon.  
- **Attendance Tracking:** Employees must clock in/out using the internal HR portal.  
- **Absence Reporting:** Notify HR or manager via email at least 1 hour before work if unable to attend.

### Remote Work Rules

- Employees must ensure a **stable internet connection**.  
- **Confidential data** must not be shared using personal accounts.  
- **VPN and company-approved devices** are mandatory when accessing internal tools.

---

## 4. Leave Policies

### Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive **20 days** of paid leave per year.  
- Leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least **7 days** in advance.  
- Unused leaves may carry over up to **10 days** to the next year.

### Sick Leave
- Up to **10 days** per year with a doctor’s certificate if absent for more than 2 consecutive days.

### Maternity & Paternity Leave
- **Maternity Leave:** 16 weeks paid.  
- **Paternity Leave:** 10 working days.

### Compassionate Leave
- 3 days for the loss of immediate family members.

### Unpaid Leave
- May be granted at management discretion for extended absences.

---

## 5. Compensation & Benefits

- **Salary Payment:** Processed monthly via bank transfer by the 5th working day.  
- **Bonus:** Annual performance bonuses based on appraisal results.  
- **Provident Fund:** Employees contribute 10% of basic salary, matched by the company.  
- **Medical Coverage:** Employees and dependents are covered under a group health plan.  
- **Professional Development:** Financial assistance for relevant courses or certifications.

---

## 6. Performance Review & Promotion

Performance appraisals occur **annually** and consider:
- Technical performance and quality of work  
- Collaboration and communication  
- Adherence to company values  
- Initiative and innovation  

### Promotion Policy
Promotions are based on **merit and performance**, not tenure.  
Recommendations must be approved by **HR** and the **Department Head**.

---

## 7. Code of Conduct & Workplace Behavior

Employees are expected to:
- Maintain professionalism and respect at all times.  
- Refrain from discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate behavior.  
- Use company resources responsibly.  
- Avoid conflicts of interest (e.g., working for competing firms).  
- Uphold confidentiality of client projects and internal operations.

### Dress Code
- Smart casual on weekdays; relaxed attire on Fridays.

### Social Media
- Employees must not disclose internal matters or speak on behalf of Softvence without permission.

---

## 8. Information Security & Confidentiality

- Comply with **Softvence Information Security Policy**.  
- Use only **company-authorized software and storage**.  
- Confidential data (e.g., client info, financials) must not be copied to personal devices.  
- Report any data breach or phishing attempt immediately to **security@softvence.agency**.  
- Upon resignation, all access credentials must be revoked and devices returned within **3 working days**.

---

## 9. Workplace Safety & Wellbeing

Softvence is committed to maintaining a **safe and healthy** work environment.

- **Fire drills** are conducted quarterly.  
- **Emergency exits** must remain clear at all times.  
- **First aid kits** are available on every floor.  
- **Remote employees** should maintain ergonomic setups and report injuries occurring during work hours.

### Wellbeing Programs
- Monthly wellness check-ins and mental health sessions.  
- Access to counseling through the **Employee Assistance Program (EAP)**.

---

## 10. Disciplinary Action

Violations of company policy may result in the following:

1. **Verbal Warning** – for minor issues.  
2. **Written Warning** – formal notice recorded in HR file.  
3. **Suspension or Termination** – for repeated or serious violations.

### Examples of Gross Misconduct
- Theft, fraud, or falsification of records.  
- Harassment or violence.  
- Data breach or misuse of confidential information.  
- Intentional damage to company property.

---

## 11. Exit & Resignation Policy

- Employees must provide **1 month notice** before resigning.  
- HR will conduct an exit interview and clearance process.  
- All company assets (laptops, cards, documents) must be returned.  
- Final salary processed within **10 working days** post-clearance.

---

## 12. Data Privacy Policy (Public Section)

Softvence Agency collects and processes employee data for **HR, payroll, and operational purposes**.  
The company complies with applicable data protection laws (e.g., **GDPR**).  
Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary.

---

## 13. Policy Review & Updates

This handbook is **reviewed annually** by the HR Department and approved by the CEO.  
Updates are communicated via official email and acknowledged by employees through digital signature in the HR portal.
